During the Winter of 2018 Plymouth State University rented an office in Waterville Valley's Town Square retail area to provide high school students and their families who are visiting the region to learn about the school and meet with admissions staff. This was coupled with a guerilla marketing campaign on the ski mountains near the University. The guerilla campaign logo and style was used to promote the space.

A poster sent to high school guidance counselors and rack cards that were placed in rest areas, hotels, and restaurants in the state.

Microsite that promoted the space and upcoming events. An area was included that introduced our student guerilla marketers.

Interior at Waterville Valley town center. Assisted in creation and layout as well as design of large signs.

Mobile application ad.
