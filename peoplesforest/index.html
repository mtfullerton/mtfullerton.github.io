2018

The People's Forest

Graphic Design

These materials were created for an exhibition at Plymouth State University's Museum of the White Mountains. The exhibition celebrated the centennial of the White Mountain National Forest.

The People's Forest Brochure

This diecut brochure included a timeline of events in the National Forest history among other information.

The People's Forest Posters

These posters included old photography from the National Forest's history.

Pull Up Banners

A pull banner was created both for the exhibition and the museum itself. The exhibition banner was part of a traveling display that went out to other locations across the state.

Museum of the White Mountains Rack Card

This rack card was created alongside the other materials to promote the museum itself and relay membership information.
