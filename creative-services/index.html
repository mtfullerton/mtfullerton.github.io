Saul O Sidore Lecture Series Fall 2015
Graphic Design, Resource Design

This poster and brochure series was designed for a fall lecture series being held at Plymouth State University. The lectures focus on issues and events in politics, society, and culture. The posters required a lot of text information so it was critical to give enough space for that while keeping the series interesting to the eye. It was important to keep the poster and brochure theme similar. To do this I incorporated the colored box from the poster into the brochure lecture titles. The color of the boxes for the brochure were inspired by the title text from the front of the brochure. This also helped to create a common color theme for the brochure.

2015

Evening of Connections
Graphic Design, Resource Design

This design was created for a dinner event being held for Plymouth State scholarship students and award recipients. The border had to be interesting but not detract from the main information of the invitation and RSVP. The title piece and decorations help keep the composition interesting to the viewer. The squares in the border design were also used on the mailing envelope as part of the event title.
