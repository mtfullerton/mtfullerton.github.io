High School Poster
2016 | Graphic Design, Poster Design

This poster was designed to be sent out High School guidance counselors to put up in order to interest prospective students in the Univeristy. It was important for the poster to grab attention of students. The poster detailed dates for Open House events along with main features of Plymouth State Unviersity. I incorporated the slanted lines on the mailing envelope along with the subtitle present on the poster.
