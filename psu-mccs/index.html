Infosheets
2016 | Graphic Design, Resource Design

These infosheets were designed for department heads to create their own infosheets for their majors and minors. There are versions for both Undergraduate and Graduate courses with two versions for Graduate, two sided and one sided, depending on how much information was avaliable. After completion they had to be set up into versions accesible for proffesors unfamilair with Adobe programs. I also had to create Microsoft Word versions that could be used by proffesors who didn't have Adobe programs.
