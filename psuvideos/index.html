A PSU Tradition: Pumpkins on Rounds Tower

Video Filming and Editing | 2020

One of Plymouth State's most popular traditions is the pumpkins on Rounds Tower. PSU Faculty, Staff, and students were interviewed on the tradition and how they think the pumpkins get up there. This video was produced for social media around Halloween.

Fall 2020 Opening Series

Video Filming and Editing | 2020

A series of videos created to provide information Fall 2020 opening plans due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Multiple faculty members and a student were interviewed to help students and familes understand expectations for re-opening. The series includes a animated video providing six tips to keep the community healthy and safe. This series was shared on social media and on the main website.
