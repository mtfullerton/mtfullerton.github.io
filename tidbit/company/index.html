Our Company

Tidbit Crackers is a company concerned with the way we create our products. With the consumer in mind we want our crackers to taste great but also be healthy so our consumers can feel great eating them. By reading further you can get a good look at the basis our company as well as thoughts from members of our team.

Our People

Our company is made from our people. They define every aspect of our mission statement and seek to provide you only with the very best. Each part of the cracker making process employs the people who make up our amazing team. We have a direct connection to our wheat and flavor farmers. Farmers like Ben Irwile supply us with natural ingredients as well as giving us his opinion on how we make our crackers. "The people at Tidbit Crackers are incredibly proficient. They are genuinely interested in us as farmers and respect us as much as their own team members."

Ben Irwile Wheat Farmer

"I have been a part of this company since it's off springing and each day it never fails to amaze me how dedicated our team members are to providing our consumers with the very best."

Our Guarantee

We hold all of our products to the highest standard. When we say 100% natural flavor we mean it. Our guarantee to you as a consumer is give you crackers that not only have amazing taste but also that this flavor comes directly from nature. We are cautious about our process We don't extract any nutrients from our base ingredients and therefore we don't have to add superfluous flavors in after. None of our ingredients have been genetically modified. If you choose Tidbit Crackers we guarantee you will be satisfied.
