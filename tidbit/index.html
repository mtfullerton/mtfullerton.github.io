Tidbit Crackers

Graphic and Product Design

Web Design and Development

My senior BFA thesis project. I created a company that produces specialty crackers. The logo was created by hand instead of using a specific font. The biggest design element was the stamp like imagery that was present on the front and back of the boxes as well as present in the backgrounds of many of the pieces. The photography was taken and edited by me for use in the printed materials and website.

Design of front and back of boxes for each flavor. Final image shows how the boxes open using the logo as a flap.
