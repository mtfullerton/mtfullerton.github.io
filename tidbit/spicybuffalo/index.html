Spicy
Buffalo

This flavor is perfect for those looking for something hot. These crackers have been spiced up by flakes of red hot pepper. Perfectly paired with a solid base of cool cheese or fruit the heat from these crackers will leave you wanting more. Each crunch will bring a joy to your taste buds and to your healthy lifestyle.

Nutritional Values
  • 100% Whole Grain
  • All Natural Flavors
Great Pairings
  • Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese
  • Fresh Herb and Cream Spread
  • Fruity Zinfandel or Cabernet Sauvignon
