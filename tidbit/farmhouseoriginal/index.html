Farmhouse Original

Our flagship flavor. This delicious cracker utilizes the power of 100% Whole Grain to provide a perfect platter for your favorite toppings. However these crackers are tasty by themselves with a natural flavor that will make you smile. These hints of seasoning will make each crunch both a joy to your taste buds and to your healthy lifestyle.

Nutritional Values
  • 100% Whole Grain
  • All Natural Flavors
Great Pairings
  • Gouda cheese and green apples
  • Salami and Brie cheese
  • Cherry tomatoes, basil, and herbed cheese
